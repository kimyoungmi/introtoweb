Hello! This is Youngmi Kim!

My work is about healing and sewing is my medium. I find the process of sewing to be very grounding as it connects me to some deep, unspoken, place inside myself that I then translate into the visual language of my black and white textured canvases. My work is unconsciously organized on the flat surface, traversed and punctured by needles and thread. The repetitive motion within this organic process serves as a sort of meditation that initiates a conversation between myself and needle and thread in art.


Youngmi Kim is from Seoul and live in new york.
